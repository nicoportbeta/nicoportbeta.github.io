The Website realized for the Communication Design Final Synthesis Studio Exhibition in BASE Milano. Using the left mouse click on an empty space will let the user freely paint the background of the page. This feature is a digital translation of the visual identity of the exhibition, which is based on the airbrush paint effect. The website initially served as a teaser. Afterwards, it has been expanded with photos and a video reportage of the event.

Title taken from the teaser video

The exhibition emphasizes on the concretization of abstract words through the means of communicative devices, and the relationship between the given brief (one "state of being" assigned to each group) and the final outcome (a Communication Design project, each with a visual identity created analogically). Considering the anti-disciplinary nature of the projects, the website needed to reflect this concept and adapt it to an interactive media.
A "raw" brutalist visual language was chosen and coupled with a simple interactive experience to reiterate the importance of the physical gestures in moving from words to works.

Wireframe

  1. Texts “above the fold” only contain essential informations which will then be expanded on the second portion of the page.
  2. At the first mouse scroll the screen will stay still, while the reportage video will slide from the bottom, overlapping the other elements of the page.
  3. Left clicking on the webiste empty areas will allow the user to paint the background with spray paint, whose trail will fade after a couple of seconds.
  4. In the third section there’s a list of the words used as a starting point for each group and the corresponding project name. Moving the mouse on each text line will show a photo preview of the project on the right.
  5. The navigation bar on the right grants a quick access to every section of the site.
